## Key Campaign Notes

1. **Landing Page:** Every post drives to innovativeimagesphotos.com/corporate-portraits
2. **CTA:** Schedule a phone consultation (low commitment, high conversion intent)
3. **No "you/your" language:** All posts speak about the transformation, the organization, and the outcome — never directly at the reader
4. **Hook variety across all 12 posts:** Pain point (3), Aspiration (2), Social proof (3), Urgency (2), Educational (1), Community (1)
5. **Local SEO reinforcement:** McKinney, Dallas, Plano, Frisco, Allen, DFW, and North Texas are mentioned consistently across all posts
6. **Instagram "Link in bio" CTA** is used on all four Instagram posts — ensure the Linktree or bio link points directly to the landing page during this campaign
7. **Brand consistency:** Every post reinforces the 30+ years of experience, the McKinney location, and the professional-yet-approachable brand voice

## Recommended Campaign Duration

Deploy these 12 posts across a 3-week window, staggering platforms so content doesn't overlap on the same day across all three channels. This maintains consistent visibility without audience fatigue.
